Our story, crafted with care

Founded with a love for thoughtful design and quality, Liora creates pieces meant to be lived in — stylish, comfortable, and made to last.

Who we are

Liora began as a small studio with a simple mission: to make clothing that blends timeless style with everyday comfort. Over the years we've grown into a team of designers, makers, and storytellers who care deeply about craftsmanship and sustainability.

Our mission

To design high-quality fashion with purpose — pieces that feel good, look great, and are responsibly made for the modern wardrobe.
